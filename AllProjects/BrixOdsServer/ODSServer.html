About product

Brix ODS Server Portal is the product of iASYS Technology solutions Pvt Ltd. It is designed for system configurations, to keep all the track of ODS server. It includes functionalities related to ODS like License Files , Configurations , Logs , System Information. Brix ODS Server Portal application helps to maintain servers and quick operations related to ODS.
