About

Srushti dental Hospital Management is an application designed to maintain the internal management of the Hospital. The application has all the features which make management more easy and quick. The application includes employees, patients, and other business management.




Problem Statements

Problems in exesting management

Problem

The existing process of managing all the information is so hectic. all the data is stored in excel and on paper which gets more complicated to manage and maintain.

Problem

At the end of the day, we have to maintain and look after today`s accounts and details of patients and have to maintain all the documents. this process is much more time-consuming.

Problem

Fetching previous data of patients is also a big problem it requires a lot of time and effort, also we have to look after appointments which are done, pending, or in a processsing state.

Pain Points





Pain Points

  • We have to maintain all the documents and excel sheets all day for management.
  • To get information on appointments that have been done, pending, processed, and other things we have to go through the case paper every time.
  • Management of staff and looking after all the staff get complicated, keeping track of all the staff is important too.
  • Keeping track of appointments is also one of the hectic tasks. We need a calendar which shows all the appointments against specific staff.
