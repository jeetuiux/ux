About Orbit EV

Orbit is the product of iASYS Technology Solutions Pvt Ltd. Orbit is the platform, where vehicle testing is done. Vehicle is tested on the test bed , which has number of sensors mounted on it. This sensor generates the bulk amount of data which is analysed later. Orbit EV is project based on electric vehicle testing and data Analysis.
